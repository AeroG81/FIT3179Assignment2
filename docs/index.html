FORMULA 1

Formula One (also known as Formula 1 or F1) is the highest class of international auto racing for single-seater formula racing car sanctioned by Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). The World Drivers' Championship, which became the FIA Formula One World Championship in 1981, has been one of the premier forms of racing around the world since its inaugural season in 1950.

Constructor Ranking (1960-2020)

Since Mercedes return as the frontline F1 constructor in 2010, they have been the most dominating team of modern Formula 1. Red Bull's arrival into Formula 1 in 2005 have some consecutive wins from 2010 - 2013, since then they have been fighting to hold their ground amongst the top teams. In 2021, Red Bull is one of the strongest constructor rival of Mercedes.
