FORMULA 1

Formula One (also known as Formula 1 or F1) is the highest class of international auto racing for single-seater formula racing car sanctioned by Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). The World Drivers' Championship, which became the FIA Formula One World Championship in 1981, has been one of the premier forms of racing around the world since its inaugural season in 1950.
